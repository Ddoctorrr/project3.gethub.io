about us

  • An overview of the business

    • An e-commerce where clients can buy services such as:

    • Online shopping
    • Web development
    • SEO
    • Advertisements
    • Marketing
    • Logo design
    • Graphic design
    • ETC...

  • Goals and objectives of the design project

    • Our vision is to create a website that is easy to use and easy to customize. In addition to that, we want a user-friendly and safe website where we can have discussions and contact with clients. A website safe to use so clients can pay via our webpage for the service desired.

  • The target audience and market

    • Small and Medium business owners like shops willing to transfer their brand online, small businesses with low-ranking websites, or businesses struggling to have consistent sales.

  • Keywords for how the organization would like to be perceived

    • A tool for businesses that allows them to increase their growth, whether it is in sales or in opening multiple franchises.

  • The competition

    • Our competition includes other service-based businesses and freelancer platforms such as:

    • FIVER
